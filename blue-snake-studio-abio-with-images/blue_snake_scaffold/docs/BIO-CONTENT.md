## Core identity

Tom Carroll / The Moss Man is a hands-on creator, studio builder, storyteller, skater, singer, painter, poet, songmaker, digital developer, behavioural thinker and self-declared pirate wizard.

## Connected studio system

Blue $nake Studio is not one project. It is a connected creative system:

- Meta-Pet teaches.
- Moss 60 gives it structure.
- Auralia gives it myth.
- Black Wing Crew gives it sound.
- The Teacher’s Secret Cheatsheet gives it classroom use.
- The Grammarian’s Coup Memetic Lexicon gives it language.

## Positioning

Blue $nake Studio builds art, music and apps from the same source: pattern.

The art gives it a face.  
The music gives it a voice.  
The apps give it a body.

## Meta-Pet promise

Children should not have to trade privacy for learning. Meta-Pet is framed as a free, offline, zero-data-collecting, privacy-first STEAM learning tool with no ads, no trackers, no unnecessary student accounts and no admin-heavy setup.

## Moss 60

Moss 60 explores number as visual, audible, symbolic and interactive. It supports digital DNA, identity-glyph logic, visual mathematics, hepta-encryption concepts and pattern-based learning.

## Teacher tools

The Teacher’s Secret Cheatsheet and behavioural support planning resources are designed to equip teachers without adding to their already stretched workload, with attention to early-years support, resilience, duties of care and practical language around behavioural concerns.

## Black Wing Crew

Black Wing Crew / Neon Venom is the sound wing of the studio: dark chant, sea-shanty metal, black birds, black butterflies, omen language, recursive hooks, taiko rhythm, lyric posters, QR assets and bilingual learning sheets.

## Memetics

The Grammarian’s Coup Memetic Lexicon explores framing, repetition, suggestion, narrative control and how people can be shaped by the words their thinking uses.
