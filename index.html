Bio

I am a hands-on creator whose work sits somewhere between art, mathematics, music, mythology, education, child development and digital invention.

What started as a wide spread of hobbies has grown into the creative force behind Blue $nake Studio: a full world of symbols, systems, songs, learning tools, visual language and original ideas. I build, fix, draw, fold, write, compose, skate, experiment and follow patterns until they turn into something alive.

I have a deep interest in geometry, number, pure mathematics, music, language, behaviour and how ideas shape people before they notice they are being shaped. Without pumping my own shoes too hard, my work has become a strange but practical blend of creative maths, behaviour support, visual storytelling, educational design and mythic branding.

“Technology should give attention back, not steal it.”

That idea sits underneath Meta-Pet, the teacher resources, the memetic writing and the wider Blue $nake Studio direction.

Meta-Pet · BlueSnakeStudios.com

Meta-Pet is the flagship Blue $nake Studio digital companion concept: local-first, child-safe, privacy-first, school-aware and built around healthy device use.

Not a pet. A process.

Meta-Pet is designed as an offline-first learning companion with no ads, no trackers, no default student accounts and no unnecessary cloud collection. It is framed as a STEAM tool — Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics — built for curiosity, resilience, creativity and calm learning.

Core promise

  • Local-first by design
  • Privacy-first learning
  • Healthy screen use
  • Teacher-friendly lessons
  • No admin burden

Evolve

Moss 60 · Visual Maths

Through my own mathematical theory, I developed the foundation for Moss 60: a number-based system that makes mathematics visual, audible, symbolic and interactive.

The system connects base-60 thinking, 108 and 360 cycles, geometry, sound, identity marks, companion DNA, and layered security concepts. It is not just calculation; it is a visual and sonic way to feel number as structure.

In the Meta-Pet world, this becomes verifiable uniqueness, identity-glyph logic, layered security ideas and pattern-based learning.

Teaching / Behaviour Support

Teacher’s Secret Cheatsheet

A practical behaviour-support and classroom resource strand designed to equip teachers without adding to their already stretched workload. The focus is early-years support, resilience, healthy behaviour, duties of care, and giving young people the best chance to grow.

Built for real classrooms

No logins, no setup maze, no admin tech overhead. Lessons and supports should be available when a teacher actually needs them.

Behavioural lens

The work considers the legal meaning, duties and responsibilities involved when supporting children with behavioural concerns and challenges.

Attention-first design

The app should not steal a child’s attention. It should give attention back through curiosity, agency and learning.

Black Wing Crew · Neon Venom

Black Wing Crew is a dark chant / sea-shanty / pop-metal / mythic lyric project built from oars, birds, black butterflies, omen language, recursive hooks and crew energy.

Core language

Black birds dream black butterflies. The sign dreams the seer. The seer dreams the sign. Horror wears the shape of a circle with one side.

Sonic world

Dark pop-metal chant, rowing call-and-response, taiko pulse, cinematic moon-over-bay atmosphere and hook-based memetic repetition.

Output system

Lyrics, posters, QR campaigns, video prompts, promo copy, bilingual learning sheets and shareable music-world assets.
